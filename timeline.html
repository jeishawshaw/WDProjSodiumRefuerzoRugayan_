2006 – The first album

Taylor releases her debut album, Taylor Swift, after signing with Big Machine Records!

2009 – First Grammy Win

Fearless wins Album of the Year, making her the youngest winner of the award!

2010 – Owning her voice

Speak Now is written entirely by Taylor alone, which is Taylor's Version.

2012 – Growing pain

Red captures heartbreak, but at the same time change, and musical exploration.

2014 – The pop reinvention

1989 marks her full transition into becoming a pop artist!

2017 – Reputation

Taylor reclaims her narrative after getting hate online, in her album reputation.

2019 – Healing & freedom

Lover celebrates her self-acceptance and personal growth

2020 – Reinvention

folklore and evermore released during the pandemic, redefines her artistry, even during hard times.

2022 – Sleepless Nights (Literally)

Midnights breaks records across global charts!

2023 – The Eras Tour

This was the highest-grossing tour in music history.
